Birth-day Detector

Let's have some fun, want to know what day of the week you were born which will result to your Akan name as well?

Click the find out button below on inputting your birthday date details in the form.

Gender:

The majority tend to know their birthday date and not the day of the week they were born. And so,this web application allows one to key in their birth date and calculates the day of the week one was born and depending on the gender selected outputs their Akan name.


Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.
